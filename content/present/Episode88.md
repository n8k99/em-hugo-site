<p>This Episode was recorded in the time window between 31 December and 2 January. The first track End of Days is a Trip Hop/Ambient composition made in the morning, also the last song of last year. Then starting with Ionian Columns, I begin inventing a hybrid between improvisational jazz compositions and DnB tempo pushes. Furiously Falling Forward and Bartender Calls continued this evolution where I used some production techniques to take the composition into an area where my physical abilities were exceeded. For the final track, Ballistic Blues, i really dove into this technique of playing the song extremely slowly while recording and then speeding it up much faster during the mixing process.
